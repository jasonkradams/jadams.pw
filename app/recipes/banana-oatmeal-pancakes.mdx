## Ingredients

- 1½ cups rolled oats (gluten-free)
- 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 egg white
- 1 banana
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- ¼–½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla (optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine almond milk, eggs, egg white, banana, maple syrup, vanilla, oats, baking powder, and salt in a blender.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Heat skillet over medium heat; spray with cooking spray or add coconut oil/butter.
4. Pour batter into round circles; cook 2–3 minutes per side until golden.
5. Drizzle with maple syrup and desired toppings.

## Tips

- Use half steel cut oats for added texture.
- Top with fresh berries, chocolate chips, banana slices, or sliced almonds.
- These also work great as waffles — just pour the batter into a preheated waffle iron.
